How to learn Korean Alphabet fast?

The challenge before us is the task of memorizing 40 symbols of the Korean alphabet, their writing, and pronunciation. At first glance, this seems rather complicated, but if you follow a professional methodology, everything is learned very quickly and easily.

The groups division of The Korean alphabet into small groups for the convenience step-by-step memorization:

We will divide 40 letters of the Korean alphabet into small groups of 4-6 characters. Such blocks are easy to memorize.

The Korean Alphabet Composition

In the Korean language there are 24 letters (14 consonants, 10 vowels), 11 diphthongs (complex vowels), and 5 double consonants:

  • 14 simple consonants: ㄱㄴㄷㄹㅁㅂㅅㅇㅈㅊㅋㅌㅍㅎ
  • 11 diphthongs: ㅐㅒㅔㅖㅘ ㅙ ㅚ ㅝ ㅞ ㅟ ㅢ
  • 5 doubled consonants: ㄲㄸㅃㅆㅉ
